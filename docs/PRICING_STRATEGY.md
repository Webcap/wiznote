# WizNote Premium Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Recommended Pricing:** $9.99/month or $79.99/year  
**Target Market:** Students and casual professionals  
**Profit Margin:** 77% (~$7.69 per user/month)  
**Break-even:** 130 premium users/month  

---

## 💰 Cost Analysis

### Monthly Cost Per Premium User

```
AI Feature Costs (average usage):
├─ Voice Recording: 60 min × $0.006 = $0.36
├─ AI Transcription: 10 × $0.006 = $0.06
├─ AI Summaries: 15 × $0.010 = $0.15
├─ AI Key Details: 15 × $0.001 = $0.02
├─ AI Quizzes: ~20 × $0.030 = $0.60
├─ AI Flashcards: ~20 × $0.005 = $0.10
└─ AI Name Generation: 10 × $0.001 = $0.01
                                     ─────
Base AI Cost:                        $1.30/user

Additional Costs:
├─ Database/Storage: $0.20/user
├─ Infrastructure: $0.10/user
└─ Stripe fees (3%): Variable based on price
                                     ─────
Total Cost:                          $1.60-2.00/user/month
```

**Key Insight:** Even with unlimited AI usage, costs remain manageable at ~$2/user/month.

## 🎯 Pricing Psychology

### Why $9.99/month Works

**1. Under $10 Barrier**
- Psychologically feels "affordable"
- "Less than $10" is easier to justify
- Avoids sticker shock

**2. Coffee Comparison**
- "Less than 2 coffees per month"
- Makes price feel insignificant
- Relatable comparison

**3. Annual Discount**
- $79.99/year = $6.66/month (33% off)
- Drives long-term commitment
- Reduces churn
- Predictable revenue

**4. Student Budget-Friendly**
- Manageable for college students
- Less than textbook rental
- Competes with Spotify/Netflix pricing

**5. Premium Positioning**
- Not too cheap (maintains quality perception)
- Not too expensive (accessible to masses)
- Sweet spot for SaaS tools

### Marketing Messages

```
Primary Message:
"Unlimited AI-powered studying for less than $10/month"

Supporting Messages:
├─ "Less than 2 coffees for unlimited AI study tools"
├─ "Save 33% with annual plan - just $6.66/month"
├─ "Everything unlimited: Quizzes, flashcards, transcription, and more"
└─ "Join 1,000+ students crushing their goals with AI"
```
